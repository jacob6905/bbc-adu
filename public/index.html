Process

A clear path from idea to move-in.

  2. Scope & Estimate Define the build approach, major trades, finishes, schedule, and budget range.

  • Garage and existing-space conversions
  • Kitchen and bathroom buildouts

  • Coordination with plans, permits, and inspections
